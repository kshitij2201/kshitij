EduReach Abroad are an Foreign Education Consulting Company based in Nagpur. We specialise in offering affordable education opportunities across the globe since 2018. We have a well set up office in the happening area of Nagpur with trained staff. EduReach Abroad is promoted by Mr. Pradeep Meshram a B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from the prestigious IIT Bombay. He has a vast experience in the field of education in various capacities in institutes like Govt Polytechnic, VNIT Nagpur and IILM Business, Nagpur. He is connected with many social organisations and very well known for students career counselling. Nagpur is in the heart of India with a close boundary with Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Telangana and Orissa. Lot of students from these states come to Nagpur for studies. Our focus is on the students from smaller cities and towns where adequate awareness about career opportunities abroad is very less.
